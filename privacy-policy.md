# Privacy Policy for Stashly

Last updated: May 2, 2026

## What data we collect
- URLs and page titles of links you save
- Account authentication tokens stored locally in your browser
- Page metadata (title, description, keywords) from saved links

## How we use your data
- To save and retrieve your links
- To generate semantic embeddings for search
- To authenticate your account

## Data storage
- Your links are stored securely in our database
- Authentication tokens are stored locally in chrome.storage.local
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not share your data with anyone

## Third party services
- We use Google Gemini API to generate search embeddings
